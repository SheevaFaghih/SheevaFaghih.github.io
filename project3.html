Project 3

This third project dealt with competing in a case competition that focused on fixing unforeseen accounting issues.

This assignment was interesting because as an accounting student, I see most accounting laws as concrete but further learned that this is not the case. My two partners and I were required to present in front of our other classmates as well as three judges. After the presentation, judges asked questions to verify the depth of our understanding.
