Project 3

This third project dealt with competing in a case competition that focused on fixing potential/unforeseen accounting issues.

This assignment was interesting because as an accounting student, I see most accounting laws as concrete but further learned that this is not the case. My two partners and I were required to present in front of our other classmates as well as three judges. We then opened the floor to questions which allowed the judges to test if we actually were familiar with the information that we were presenting.
