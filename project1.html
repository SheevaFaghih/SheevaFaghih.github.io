Project 1

This was an assignment based on auditing the financials of a public company.

Though I have chosen to become a tax accountant, I had the opportunity to take an audit course at UT Dallas. I applied what I learned throughout this course into a project where I had to audit a specific account in the financials of a company of my choice. My partner and I decided on the company Hershey and we chose to audit the PP&E account.
